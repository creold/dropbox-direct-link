Dropbox Direct Link Generator — Fast and Secure

What does this generator do?

Standard shared links generated by Dropbox point to a web preview page. To download files, your users or external scripts must visit this page and manually click the download button.

Our utility instantly converts these URLs into two highly practical direct formats:

  • Direct Download: One-click download links that bypass the Dropbox interface entirely.
  • Direct View: Links for embedding media assets (images, audio, documents) directly on your site or app.

Privacy First

We do not collect, store, or analyze your files and links. The entire conversion process occurs exclusively inside your browser via client-side JavaScript.

Your original links never reach our servers and are never shared with third parties. Usage is completely autonomous, confidential, and safe.

How to copy links from Dropbox?

You can obtain the necessary shared link in two simple ways:

Via Web Browser:

  1. Hover over the desired file inside your Dropbox dashboard.
  2. Click the Share button.
  3. In the popup modal, click Copy link.

Via Desktop Application:

  1. Open your local Dropbox folder in File Explorer (Windows) or Finder (macOS).
  2. Right-click the file you wish to share.
  3. Select Copy Dropbox link from the context menu.

Preparing TXT or CSV for Import

For processing hundreds of items in batch mode, you can upload file lists:

  • TXT File: A simple text file with one original Dropbox shared link pasted on each new line.
  • CSV File: Upload a spreadsheet. Our local script will automatically scan all cells, extract the Dropbox links, remove any duplicates, and prepare a structured table.
